macOS, 2024, Apple Park, Wallpaper

Apple Park Wallpaper

A collection of nine Mac & iPad backgrounds featuring beautiful locations around Apple Park.

In macOS Sonoma, Apple added the systemwide API to add custom backgrounds in apps (FaceTime, Photo Booth, Zoom, etc.) using the camera on the Mac. And in an early beta of macOS Sequoia, nine background images of locations across Apple Park were bundled with this new feature. The images feature several iconic locations around the campus, including the rainbow arch, courtyard fountain, fitness center, and the entranceway of Apple Park.

A collection of nine Mac & iPad backgrounds featuring beautiful locations around Apple Park.

Thanks to Remo (@remo_Pr0) for finding the directory that stores these images. But at 1920 × 1080, the images were fine as Facetime backgrounds but far too small for anything else. But thanks to some upscaling severe voodoo, I got the files looking pretty remarkable, even after increasing their size by nearly 10x from the original. As someone who has never visited Apple Park, I find these images give a rare glimpse of some of this campus's beautiful architecture and landscaping.The wallpapers are best suited for landscape viewing and are available for the Mac & iPad. Enjoy!
